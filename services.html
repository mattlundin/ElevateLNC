SERVICES

(Yes, we do more than chart reviews)

Legal nurse consultants (LNC) help legal professionals optimize their time and resources. Focus your efforts on crafting your case and less time dredging through endless medical records.

MEDICAL RECORD PREPARATION

Navigating stacks of medical records can be daunting. We understand. Luckily, deciphering medical records is our specialty! Our healthcare professionals will comb through your case medical records and create a succinct yet comprehensive timeline and chronology report. Your “blueprint” includes hyperlinking resources, bookmarking, OCR and bates stamping. Our bread and butter is identifying the gaps, and helping you navigate the facts as easily as possible.

MERIT REVIEW

Save time and energy with a Merit Review. Knowing whether or not your case has merit, based on medical facts, is critical. A Merit Review by an expert healthcare professional sets the tone for your case strategy and execution.

MEDICAL RESEARCH

Does your case need a little boost? A strong case relies on strong evidence. Let the nurse experts at ELNC support your case through evidence based research. We will do the digging and ensure your case strategy is supported with credible evidence.

MEDICAL RECORD AUDIT

Identifying discrepancies within the medical chart is critical to your case. Whether you are a plaintiff or defendant attorney, understanding the patient's medical record allows for optimal client representation. Let the experts at ELNC perform an audit on your medical records with a keen clinical eye. Whether electronic or paper chart, maximize your representation with a professional medical record analysis, and review.

BILLING SUMMARIES

It’s not a secret that healthcare has its own language. Jump into the throes of billing and coding, now you're in another sublanguage with changing dialects. (Looking at you, ICD codes!) ELNC will take the voyage through the medical records, extract the billing summary and create a succinct resource that is easy to navigate and decipher what remains. The billing summary links with the chronology report, where you have additional support via hyperlinking, bates stamping and a blueprint of what is in your records.

MEDICAL EXPERT WITNESS & SUBJECT MATTER EXPERT (SME)

Need to support your case with medical professional testimony? ELNC will pair you with the best medical expert witness or nurse subject matter expert to support your case.
