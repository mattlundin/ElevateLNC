Sentinel Event: Patient Falls

July 05, 2022

If you are just joining in on our Sentinel Series, you may be wondering what exactly is a Sentinel Event?


A Sentinel Event is a patient safety event not primarily related to the natural course of the patient's illness or underlying condition that results in death, permanent harm, severe harm, temporary harm and/or requires intervention to sustain life.
